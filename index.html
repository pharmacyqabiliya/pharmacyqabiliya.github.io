About us


Pharmacy Qabiliya is a registered pharmacy founded in 2023 in Martil MOR 🇲🇦 by Dr Yassine El Jebari a qualified and experienced pharmacist with B.Pharm from the University of Granada Spain.

Pharmacy Qabiliya is much more than a dispenser of medicines. Our patients and customers are our main focus and we endeavour to provide them with high quality healthcare products and services, including blood pressure and diabetes testing for chronic conditions, and cholesterol and heart checks.

You can shop all your private prescriptions, medicines and favourite parapharmacie & beauty products in our store in Martil. You can also order by whatsapp and pick up and pay in store.
